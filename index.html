dear maddie,

so at the start of summer i was laid off and decided to go all in on the music stuff. i used all the money i had saved to work with a guy i thought would be a long term partner. i paid to use his studio to make what was going to be my debut project, doing the wrong thing. but he took my money and stole my files. i trusted him blindly despite some red flags and paid the price. it's silly but i thought contracts would muddy the waters. to make matters worse my hard drive died so i lost all the music i was working on.

still i gave it my all and that is exactly what i set out to do. i lost myself and it literally cost me everything. i was so obsessed i let my relationships, mental and physical health slip. anyway, i learned some valuable lessons 1) how you get there is what you get there, 2) if there’s no love it’s no good and 3) don’t be so impulsive. i don't really have anywhere to go but i'm sure when i get there i'll know. i've got my guitar, a few empty notebooks and lots of time. figure i'll hit the road, no expectations, just go with the flow. whatever happens, happens.
